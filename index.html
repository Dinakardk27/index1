A Questions Generator !

A Questions Generator is a tool or platform that automatically creates questions based on user input. You simply enter a topic or set some parameters, and the platform generates a variety of questions related to that input. It's useful for educators, content creators, and anyone needing questions for quizzes, discussions, or interviews, saving time and enhancing creativity.
